KOBE BRYANT

"Hard work outweighs talent - every time"

Here's a time line of Kobe Bryant's life:

  • August 23, 1978 - Kobe Bryant is born in Philadelphia, Pennsylvania, to Joe Bryant and Pam Bryant.
  • 1984 - Kobe moves to Italy when his father Joe Bryant continues his basketball career playing in the Italian league.
  • 1991 - Kobe returns to the United States and attends Lower Merion High School in Ardmore, Pennsylvania.
  • 1996 - Kobe Bryant declares his eligibility for the NBA Draft directly from high school.
  • June 26, 1996 - Kobe Bryant is selected 13th overall by the Charlotte Hornets in the NBA Draft, then traded to the Los Angeles Lakers.
  • 1996-1997 NBA Season - Bryant makes his NBA debut with the Los Angeles Lakers at the age of 18.
  • 1999-2002 - Kobe Bryant wins three consecutive NBA championships with the Los Angeles Lakers alongside Shaquille O'Neal.
  • 2004 - Kobe Bryant releases his rap album titled "K.O.B.E."
  • 2008 - Bryant wins the NBA MVP award for the first time in his career.
  • 2009 and 2010 - Kobe Bryant leads the Lakers to consecutive NBA championships, earning Finals MVP honors both times.
  • 2012 - Bryant becomes the youngest player in NBA history to reach 30,000 career points.
  • 2013 - Bryant suffers a season-ending Achilles tendon injury.
  • 2014 - Bryant announces his retirement from basketball at the end of the 2015-2016 NBA season.
  • 2016 - Kobe Bryant concludes his NBA career with a historic 60-point farewell game against the Utah Jazz.
  • January 26, 2020 - Kobe Bryant tragically dies in a helicopter crash in Calabasas, California, along with his daughter Gianna and seven others.
  • 2020 - Bryant is posthumously inducted into the Naismith Memorial Basketball Hall of Fame.
